My Pinterest Uploader App

Quickly upload images as pins to your Pinterest account

About the App

My Pinterest Uploader App is a simple tool that allows you to upload images directly to your Pinterest boards. You can use it to manage your pins more efficiently without manually opening Pinterest.

How it Works

  • Select an image from your device.
  • Choose the board where you want to upload it.
  • Click "Upload" and the pin will be added to your Pinterest account.

Privacy

Your images and Pinterest credentials are only used to upload pins. We do not store or share your data beyond what is required for uploading.
