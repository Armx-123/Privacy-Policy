Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 9, 2025

1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how My Pinterest Uploader App (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our app to upload pins to Pinterest.

2. Information We Collect

Our app only collects the following information:

  • Images you upload to create pins.
  • Your Pinterest username (if required for pin uploading).

No other personal information is collected.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used only to upload pins to your Pinterest account. We do not share, sell, or use your data for any other purpose.

4. Data Storage

Uploaded images may be temporarily stored on our servers for processing and then removed immediately after the pin is successfully uploaded. We do not retain your images or credentials.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data during transmission, but no method is 100% secure. Use at your own risk.
